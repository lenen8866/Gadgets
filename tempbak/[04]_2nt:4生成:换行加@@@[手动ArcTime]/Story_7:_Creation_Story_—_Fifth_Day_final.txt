Morning came.
It was the fifth day.@@@

God said,
"Let there be many living creatures in
 the water and let the birds fly.
" Then the water had
 many kinds of animals in it.@@@

God made very big
 water animals like whales.
He made very small
 water animals like goldfish.
There were starfish and seahorses.@@@

Birds spread their wings
 and flew through the air.
They began to sing.
They sang praises
 to their Creator.
The kingfisher flew back
 and forth looking for food.
The peacock spread
 its colorful tail.
The earth was
 becoming a beautiful place.@@@

Evening came.
The fifth day was done.
God saw that His
 work was very good.
